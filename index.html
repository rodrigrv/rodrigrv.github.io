My Work

Below you can view some of my academic works I've created since I've started. More to follow soon.

Morningside Project

My last project in my college career, I was tasked with making a fully functional website for a real client or organization. It had to have all elements previously learned from other projects like responsiveness, structure, and usability. It also had to include new elements like Dynamic HTML and AJAX.

Old West Animal Clinic

This is currently my favorite website. It was my final project for my Digital Communication Management class. The primary use of this website was to exhibit our ability to understand back-end development using PHP and MySQL. It has a working log-in / log-out system for new and existing users to schedule an appointment.

Sam's All Pet's Animal Clinic

Working with a partner on this project, we showed what kind of website the client could have. We optimized it for SEO and went for a clean and minimal approach. It's complete with reviews and an admin dashboard.

A Day in the Life

This was my final project for my multimedia production class. The goal was to incorporate various film techniques as well as execute proper editing procedures to produce a short clip about anything the student wanted. I chose to do a "day in the life" type of video.

How To Impress Your Friends

I was tasked with making any kind of "How To" video. It had to be short and sweet, no longer than 20 seconds. If you want to know the secret to the trick, ask me.

JavaJam Redesign

One of my projects during design & development II, I needed to redesign this website with the goal of using frameworks and making it responsive with media queries.

That Movie Place

This was my very first website. It was also my final project for design & development I. This was a fabricated movie company designed to fulfill the basic needs of understanding HTML and CSS.
